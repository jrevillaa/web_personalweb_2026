## The Challenge
Update/migrate a Moodle platform to a specific version and renew the visual layer to align it with institutional identity.

## The Solution
We executed migration + production launch:
* Migration and implementation of **Moodle 2.8**.
* Base configuration adjustments for operation.
* Implementation/presentation of a **new graphic theme**.
* Validation of navigation, roles, and courses.

## Results
* Platform operational in **Moodle 2.8** with renewed image.
* Improved perception and institutional visual consistency.
* Functional base ready for content growth.
