## The Challenge
Implement an institutional virtual classroom ensuring the platform is ready for academic operation, with guidelines and technical accompaniment.

## The Solution
Consultancy + implementation service:
* Installation and base configuration of Moodle.
* Initial parameterization (roles, categories, policies, and operation).
* Best practices recommendations for administration and support.
* Accompaniment in the launch of the “Clementina” virtual classroom.

## Results
* Operational virtual classroom (“**Clementina**”) ready for academic use.
* More organized administration base with practical guidelines.
* Start of adoption with technical support and continuity approach.
