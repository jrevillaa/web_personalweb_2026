## What we achieve
Increase adoption and retention by improving the mobile experience, especially for students with limited connectivity.

## Components

### 1. App and Configuration
* Configuration adjustments for efficient navigation.
* Simple access flows: login, courses, activities.
* Visual identity (branding) when applicable.

### 2. Offline (if applicable)
* Resource download and viewing without network.
* Offline activity tracking and subsequent synchronization.
* Optimization for data consumption.

### 3. Communication
* Notifications and reminders (depending on stack).
* Best practices for relevant messages (no spam).

## Benefits
* Greater participation from mobile.
* Reduced friction in accessing courses.
* Better learning continuity off-campus.
