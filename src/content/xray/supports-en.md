## What we offer
A support scheme for organizations where Moodle is mission-critical (universities, corporate, government), focusing on availability and continuity.

## Service Modalities

### 1. Preventive Maintenance
* Periodic review of logs and platform health.
* Minor updates (security) under controlled maintenance windows.
* Cleaning/optimization of caches and jobs, cron verification.
* Review of DB growth and storage.

### 2. Monitoring and Alerts
* Alerts for degradation (CPU, RAM, IO, DB, latency, errors).
* Thresholds defined by criticality (exams/enrolment).
* Availability and event reporting.

### 3. Incident Management
* Triage and containment (rapid mitigation).
* RCA (root cause analysis) and correction plan.
* Coordination with internal IT / cloud provider when applicable.

## Packages (referential)
* **Essential**: health + maintenance + support during business hours.
* **Business**: monitoring + windows + priority response.
* **Critical**: extended coverage, preparation for peaks, support for critical events.

## Deliverables
* Defined SLA/OLA (response and resolution times by severity).
* Monthly report (status, changes, recommendations).
* Operation and recovery runbooks.

## Result
Fewer crashes, faster recovery, and predictable operation with professional support.
