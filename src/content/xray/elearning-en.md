## Context
Government and universities often require: scalability, traceability, security, audit, and stable operation during critical periods.

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Scale and Concurrency
* Architectures for high demand (enrolment, exams, campaigns).
* Optimization and tuning to support peaks.
* Caching and session strategies for large volumes.

### 2. Compliance and Security
* Hardening, access policies, roles, and permissions.
* Audit approach: logs, traceability, and change control.
* Best practices for data protection and continuity.

### 3. Operation and Continuity
* Proven backup/restore procedures.
* Contingency plans (critical events).
* Support with SLAs and monitoring.

### 4. Inclusion and Accessibility
* Strategies for limited connectivity (mobile/offline when applicable).
* UX adjustments for teacher/student adoption.
* Course standardization and instructional design best practices (in coordination with academic area).

## Result
Moodle projects with an institutional approach: sustainable, auditable, and ready to operate at scale.
