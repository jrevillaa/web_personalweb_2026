## General Description

**WS to Q10** implements a stable and operable integration between Q10 and Moodle, oriented to institutions with high enrolment turnover.

### Technical Capabilities

1. **Synchronization by Period**
   * Executes jobs by period/semester with status control.
   * Structure creation/update rules.

2. **Normalization**
   * Handling of characters, codes, section formats.
   * Validations before applying changes.

3. **Operation and Audit**
   * Panel of last executions, times, errors, retries.
   * Log export for support and audit.
