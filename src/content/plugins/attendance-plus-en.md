## Overview

**Advance Attendance Plus** solves the problem of impersonation in attendance tracking. It uses modern browser technologies to verify the student physical presence.

### Key Features

1.  **Anti-Fraud**: Validates that the student is within the class perimeter.
2.  **Dynamic QR**: Generates codes that change in real-time, preventing photo sharing.
3.  **Offline Support**: Allows registration without internet and syncs when connected.
